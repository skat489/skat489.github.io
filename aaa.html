My AI Automation Journey

I started building an AI outbound prescreening agent for recruiting agencies who were tired of chasing cold or semi-warm leads. Instead of manually calling every candidate or lead, my agent picks up the work — it makes the call, asks a few qualifying questions, and only books a consultation if the person is actually interested.

Everything it learns from the call — interest level, answers, objections — is returned to the agency in a clean Google Sheet. This saves hours of time and helps recruiters focus on only the best-fit candidates.

It runs 24/7 and works great for follow-ups, reactivations, or completely cold outreach. I’ve already onboarded my first recruiting agency client and the results have been better than expected.

How It Works:

I use four main platforms to make this happen:

- Twilio: handles calling and call routing
- Vapi: powers the natural AI voice
- Make.com: connects all the tools and automates the logic
- Google Sheets: stores and organizes all the lead outcomes

It’s flexible, easy to customize, and can be tailored to any agency’s niche or workflow. Setup usually takes less than 48 hours.
